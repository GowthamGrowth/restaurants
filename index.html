Our Story

About Hotel Sree
Saravana Bhavan

Hotel Sree Saravana Bhavan is a well-loved vegetarian restaurant located in Periyanaickenpalayam, Coimbatore. Known for serving delicious South Indian breakfast dishes, wholesome veg meals, and flavorful veg biryani, we offer a comfortable dining experience for families and travelers.

Our goal is to serve fresh, hygienic, and affordable vegetarian food while maintaining the authentic taste of traditional South Indian cuisine.
